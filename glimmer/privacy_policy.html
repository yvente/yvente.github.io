Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 10, 2026

Thank you for choosing to use Glimmer (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you about how we collect, use, store, and protect your information. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Camera Data

When you use the App, we require access to your device's camera to provide real-time lighting effects (bokeh). Regarding this data:

  • The camera feed is processed locally on your device in real-time.
  • We do not record, store, or upload the video feed to any server.
  • The data is only used to render the visual effects on the screen.

1.2 Photo Library Access

To allow you to save the photos you take, we require permission to add photos to your photo library. Regarding this access:

  • We only write to your photo library when you explicitly tap the shutter button to take a photo.
  • We do not scan, view, or upload your existing photos from your library.
  • You can revoke this permission at any time through system settings.

1.3 App Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage data to improve the App, including:

  • Number of App launches
  • Feature usage frequency
  • Crash reports

This data is completely anonymous and does not contain any personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide real-time camera effects and photography features
  • To save your creations to your device's photo album
  • To improve App performance and user experience
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues

3. Information Sharing

We do not share your photos or camera data with any third parties. All processing is performed locally on your device.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Since all core processing happens locally on your device, your visual data remains under your control.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
